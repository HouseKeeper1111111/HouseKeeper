About the app

This app was created for comfortable managing household chores. See cute cat.

What do we mean?

The app to track household chores, set reminders for household chores, and track the amount of time spent on household chores. The idea for the app was created because of forgetting to do everyday routine household chores. For almost every person in the world it is boring, that's why people put things off and forget about them. Housekeeper will not allow you to forget about important things, which will help you avoid many problems.
